Our Medical Services

Comprehensive healthcare services for all your medical needs

Cardiology

Advanced cardiac care with cutting-edge diagnostic tools and treatment options for heart conditions including angioplasty, bypass surgery, and electrophysiology studies.

Neurology

Expert neurological care for disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system. Specialized in stroke management, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, and migraines.

Pediatrics

Comprehensive healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents in a child-friendly environment. Vaccinations, developmental assessments, and pediatric surgery available.

Radiology

State-of-the-art imaging services including MRI, CT scan, ultrasound, and X-ray. Advanced interventional radiology for minimally invasive procedures.

Dental Care

Complete dental solutions including preventive, restorative, and cosmetic dentistry. Dental implants, orthodontics, and oral surgery specialists available.

Ophthalmology

Comprehensive eye care including vision correction, cataract surgery, and retinal care. LASIK surgery, glaucoma management, and pediatric ophthalmology.
