About MediCare Plus

Committed to Excellence in Healthcare for Over 20 Years

Your Trusted Healthcare Partner

Founded in 2003, MediCare Plus has grown from a small community clinic to one of the region's most trusted multi-specialty hospitals. Our journey has been guided by a simple yet powerful mission: to provide exceptional healthcare services that combine medical excellence with compassionate care.

With state-of-the-art technology and a team of dedicated medical professionals, we've treated over 100,000 patients from across the country and abroad. Our commitment to innovation, research, and continuous improvement has made us a leader in medical care.

Our Mission

To deliver comprehensive, compassionate, and accessible healthcare services of the highest quality to all patients, while advancing medical knowledge through research and education.

Our Vision

To be the region's premier healthcare institution, recognized for clinical excellence, innovative treatments, and outstanding patient experiences that transform lives and communities.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a healthcare leader

2003

Establishment

MediCare Plus was founded as a small clinic with just 5 doctors and 20 beds, focusing on primary care and emergency services for the local community.

2008

Expansion Phase

Expanded to a 50-bed hospital with new departments including cardiology, pediatrics, and radiology. Introduced advanced diagnostic equipment.

2012

Modernization

Upgraded to a 150-bed multi-specialty hospital with ICU, NICU, and modern operation theaters. Achieved NABH accreditation for quality standards.

2018

Technological Advancement

Introduced robotic surgery, advanced cardiac catheterization lab, and telemedicine services. Became a teaching hospital with residency programs.

2023

Current Status

Now a 300-bed advanced healthcare facility with 40+ specialties, research center, and international patient services. Recognized as a center of excellence.
